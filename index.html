    About Nova

    Nova is a highly professional and experienced computer education centre based in mountainnestled Pokhara. Comprising of faculty considered the best in the industry. The courses at Nova are affordable with flexible payment schemes. Special discounts are also available for ex-students and to those who upgrade to higher course.
